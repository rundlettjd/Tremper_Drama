A Kid Like Jake

By Daniel Pearle

Performance Dates: January 11-13, 18-20

Performance Times: 7:30 pm

Performance Location: Tremper Studio Theatre (Room 120)

Synopsis: On the eve of the admissions cycle for Manhattan's most exclusive private schools, Alex and Greg have high hopes for their son Jake, a precocious four-year-old who happens to prefer Cinderella to G.I. Joe. But as the process continues, Jake's behavior becomes erratic and perplexing, and other adults in his life start to wonder whether his fondness for dress-up might be cause for concern. This story of a husband and wife struggling to do right by their son is a study of intimacy and parenthood and the fantasies that accompany both.
